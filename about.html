About tGS Creative

Built at the edges of strategy, media, and ideas.

tGS Creative exists for serious work that needs real structure around it — not noise, not theatre, not empty visibility.

What it is

A house built to make expertise legible, visible, and properly positioned.

At its core, tGS Creative is a strategy, management, and media practice. It works with subject-matter experts, public voices, founders, and emerging brands whose value is already real but whose infrastructure around that value is not yet strong enough.

The work spans the UK, West Africa, and the United States, with a recurring focus on African and diaspora voices that deserve better platforms, stronger positioning, and more deliberate long-term architecture.

Operating principle

Depth first. Visibility second. Execution always.

Every engagement is built from substance outward. The point is not to look impressive at the surface. The point is to build something that can stand once attention arrives.

“The clients I work best with are the ones who have already done the hard part — building real substance — and now need the right structure around it.”
Deyma, founder and director
The founder

Deyma Ayoade

Deyma has spent more than a decade building across Nigeria, Ireland, and the UK in roles spanning media production, operations, project management, strategy, and consulting. The through line has never been job title. It has been the ability to work inside difficult situations and move them toward clarity.

That instinct for complexity continues through postgraduate work in entrepreneurship and innovation at the University of Salford, where research and live consulting intersect rather than sit apart.

How Deyma works
  • Strategy before activity.Understand the terrain properly before deciding what movement should look like.
  • Execution without shortcuts.A polished front without structural quality underneath is not considered finished.
  • Long-game thinking.Build platforms, relationships, and systems that compound rather than spike and fade.
The philosophy

There is a difference between being visible and being properly positioned.

tGS Creative exists to close that difference. The work is less about content for its own sake and more about building the infrastructure around authority — strategy, narrative, opportunities, media, and brand language that can carry weight over time.

Seriousness

The site, the work, the recommendations, and the output all have to respect the gravity of what is being built.

Clarity

Ambition is not enough on its own. A clear structure is what turns potential into something durable.

Standard

Exceeding expectations is treated as the floor, not the flourish. That standard runs through every page and every engagement.
