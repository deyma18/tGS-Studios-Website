Featured case study

From silence to international literary positioning.

An 87-year-old Nigerian author and statesman with no digital infrastructure, decades of history, and a body of work that deserved far better positioning than it had received. The brief was not simply visibility. It was architecture.

10Literary agents targeted
3Countries in scope
60+Years of documented history
1Premium London podcast placement
  • The challengeZero digital footprint, highly valuable intellectual history, and a need to present the work credibly across multiple markets at once.
  • The strategyBuild the story architecture, prepare literary positioning, secure media credibility, and move toward representation with clarity rather than haste.
  • The executionPodcast placement in London, structured outreach planning across UK and US literary agents, and a tighter narrative frame around the author’s significance.
  • The resultA body of work that had been effectively hidden is now being positioned for the platforms and conversations it should always have been in.
